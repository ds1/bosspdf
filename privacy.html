Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2025

Introduction

BossPDF ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles information when you use it to capture webpages as PDFs.

Information Collection and Use

What We Collect:

  • Temporary access to webpage content during capture process
  • Basic browser information required for the extension to function

What We Don't Collect:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Login credentials
  • Form data
  • Cookies
  • Usage statistics

How We Use Information

The BossPDF extension only processes webpage content temporarily and locally on your device to:

  1. Capture the webpage content
  2. Convert it to PDF format
  3. Save it to your local downloads folder

Data Storage

  • All captured content is processed entirely on your local device
  • We do not store, transmit, or retain any captured content
  • PDFs are saved only to your local downloads folder
  • No data is sent to our servers or third parties

Required Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To capture the current webpage
  • downloads: To save the generated PDF
  • scripting: To execute the capture process

Third-Party Services

BossPDF does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

BossPDF does not knowingly collect or process information from users under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date.
